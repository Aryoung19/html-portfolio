The Best Movies according to Aryan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

The film takes audiences on an epic journey through space and time, exploring complex scientific concepts while delivering an emotionally resonant story.

The Social Dilemma

A Thought-provoking documentary that delves into the darker side of social media, exposing the hidden algorithms and manipulation tactics used to keep users engaged. Through interviews with former tech insiders, it vividly illustrates the impact of these platforms on society, revealing the dangers of addiction, polarization, and the erosion of privacy.

K.G.F 2

Action-packed cinematic extravaganza that seamlessly continues the saga of the first installment. Yash delivers a powerhouse performance as Rocky, the protagonist, while Sanjay Dutt adds a menacing charm as the formidable antagonist.
